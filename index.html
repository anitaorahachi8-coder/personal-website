Drake (Aubrey Drake Graham)

Drake (Aubrey Drake Graham) is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and actor born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada. He first became known in the entertainment world as an actor on the teen drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation, where he played the character Jimmy Brooks. Even though he gained early fame from acting, his true passion was music, and he eventually shifted his focus completely to building a music career. Drake began releasing mixtapes in the mid-2000s, and his breakout project So Far Gone brought him major attention and helped him sign with Lil Wayne’s Young Money Entertainment. From there, his career grew rapidly, and he became known for blending rap and R&B in a way that felt emotional, smooth, and different from traditional hip-hop at the time. His style often mixes singing with rapping, focusing on themes like relationships, fame, success, and personal struggles. Over the years, Drake has released many successful albums such as Take Care, Nothing Was the Same, and Views, which produced huge global hits like “One Dance,” “Hotline Bling,” and “God’s Plan.” He is one of the best-selling music artists of all time and has broken numerous streaming records. Drake is widely considered one of the most influential artists in modern music because of his impact on both hip-hop and pop culture.
